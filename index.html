Svelte Compare Image

A component to compare two images by dragging a slider to reveal the other image. Find the source at https://github.com/BrianMitchL/svelte-compare-image and on NPM at svelte-compare-image.

The markup, state logic, and styling were originally adapted from react-compare-image.
